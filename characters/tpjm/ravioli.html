Ravioli

Gender: male

Pronouns: he/him

Age: 7

Ravioli is the youngest Toilet Paper contestant ever! Full of cheese and covered in sauce, this little rapscallion comes from a divorced family and lives with his mom. He visits his dad weekly with a nice sandwich made with plenty of love. He doesn't know it yet, but he'll have some major daddy issues when he grows up!
