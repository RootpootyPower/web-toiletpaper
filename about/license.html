Policies for Derivative Works

Rootpooty Power! has enforced a complete prohibition of derivative works which contain any amount of content provided by Generative AI services, either directly trained off of our works or off of other non-public works. This is non-negotiable. Any infringing works will be taken down swiftly, without regards to the individual(s) responsible for it.

Violators of these Licensing Details will first be warned of their infringement. Failure to respond to communications within 14 days will result in further action being taken, once proved beyond a reasonable doubt that the author of a derivative is able to communicate. The most likely action will be a takedown request issued to the host of the derivative. Repeat offenders will have communications escalated once it poses a substantial threat to the integrity of Toilet Paper Production Team or Rootpooty Power!

In the event of a lack of attribution, actions will not escalate beyond repeat reminders of the importance of attribution. Commercial works are subject to sterner communication, especially if they do not attempt to separate themselves from Toilet Paper Production Team or Rootpooty Power!.

Rootpooty Power! is aware which contents of the work can be licensed under CC-BY-SA 4.0, and declares them as such. All other content is either credited under its respective license, or declares no license. In the case where no license is declared, it can be assumed that the material featured is protected under copyright, unless declared in external sources published by Rootpooty Power! after the work is published. To request a fully Creative Commons-compliant copy of the work, contact us at rootpooty@proton.me.

Exclusive content produced for financial backers will be initially published with all rights reserved for Rootpooty Power!. It is strictly prohibited to distribute these works until a 6 month period has expired, to which the content will be permanently relicensed under CC-BY-SA 4.0 and subsequently published on the Internet Archive.

However, any confidential production documents will not be licensed under CC-BY-SA 4.0 until it is deemed necessary to do so.

Attributing for Commentary, Derivative Works and Redistribution

If you choose to make a derivative work based on the original characters or settings present in Toilet Paper, Rootpooty Power! DEMANDS that you disclose that you have no association with Toilet Paper Production Team or Rootpooty Power!. If you wish to redistribute or rebroadcast Toilet Paper in its original form in any way, you must also disclose that you are not associated with the parties above.

Here are some adequate ways to attribute the work:

For web content, attributing a clip or section of the show as reference (embed the links within the text wherever possible, or include them separately)

Attributing the show as a whole, when using it for commentary or criticism:

or

“Toilet Paper, Season X Episode X: [episode title]” by Toilet Paper Production Team, a subsidiary of Rootpooty Power! is licensed under CC-BY-SA 4.0.

Attributing the text of the website as a source:

Citing the text of the website (MLA):
